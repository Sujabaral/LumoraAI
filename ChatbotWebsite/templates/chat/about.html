About Lumora

Lumora AI is a web-based mental health chatbot designed to promote awareness, self-reflection, and emotional wellbeing through guided conversations and educational resources.

Accessing mental health support can often feel overwhelming or inaccessible. Lumora aims to bridge this gap by offering a calm, private, and approachable space where users can explore mental health topics, assess their emotional state, and learn evidence-informed coping strategies.

⚠️
Clinical Disclaimer
Lumora AI does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment. It is not a replacement for licensed mental health professionals. In cases of severe distress or crisis, users are strongly advised to seek immediate professional or emergency assistance.

Our Mission

Lumora’s mission is to enhance mental health literacy and promote early self-awareness. The platform provides structured mental health information, guided conversations, mindfulness exercises, and reflective self-tests designed to help users better understand their emotional patterns and stress responses.

👥

Our Team

Lumora AI was developed as a university final-year project by a student with a strong interest in mental health technology and ethical AI design. The project emphasizes responsible use, user safety, and educational value.
