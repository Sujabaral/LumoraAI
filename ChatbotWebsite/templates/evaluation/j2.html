Academic Justification (Why this feedback design?)

A short post-session survey is a common evaluation approach in human-computer interaction because it captures perceived usefulness, user satisfaction, and trust. Lumora collects feedback once per session to avoid repeated answers from the same user and to keep evaluation consistent. The confidence-based trigger reduces interruptions: users are asked for feedback only after the chatbot produces sufficiently confident predictions and the session includes enough turns to form a meaningful opinion. This provides a balanced subjective evaluation alongside the objective sentiment performance reported in J1.
