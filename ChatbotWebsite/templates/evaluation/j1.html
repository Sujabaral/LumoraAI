Threats to Validity

Factors that may limit generalization of these results.

1) Small Dataset

Limited labeled samples may cause metric instability.

2) Class Imbalance

Uneven sentiment distribution affects Macro-F1 and confusion matrices.

3) Labeling Subjectivity

Human interpretation of emotional text can vary.

4) Domain Specificity

Results are specific to mental health chatbot conversations.

Mitigation: Increase labeled data, balance sentiment classes, and evaluate across multiple users and sessions.
